Alessandro Prieto Bustamante (born June 16, 2002) is an international peruvian undergraduate student. He is currently studying in Penn State University for a bachelor's degree in the field of Information Technology (IT) at the campus of Penn State Berks.

Early life

Alessandro was born in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, the son of Peruvian parents. His father, Enri Prieto, is a diplomat who oversees international relations for the country of Peru. By pure circumstance, Alessandro happened to be born and spent the first months of his life living in Malaysia while his father was stationed in the Malaysian Embassy. Before he was even a year old, he took his first flight to Lima, Peru as his father was called back to Peru.

Hobbies

Alessandro played a variety of videogames while growing up, he continues to pursue this hobby of his in the present time, videogames have accompanied him since his early childhood and he grew fond of them as a result, being the main activity he decides to spend his free time in. He spends his time with the friends he's made along the way in digital platforms like discord even if he is unable to meet them irl he always make sure to share his passions and play videogames with his closest friends!

He grew up to share his passion for videogames with the world, more specifically, to his close friends. As he was the creator of an online space called "The Epic Gamers" where he and his friends would connect daily to do online gaming for years, the mastermind used the tools of the platform known as discord to provide a safe space for his friends and keep in touch with them, reuniting with some old faces and meeting some new ones, this space will eventually lead him to meet his first and current partner.
